# CPD Requirements for Ghanaian Professionals in 2024

Continuing Professional Development (CPD) has become a cornerstone of professional practice in Ghana. For many regulated professions, CPD is not optional — it is a **mandatory requirement for licence renewal, certification, and career progression**.

At **Hedge Resource Centre (HRC) Ghana**, we have helped hundreds of professionals meet their CPD obligations through tailored training programmes. This guide covers everything you need to know about CPD requirements in Ghana for 2024 and beyond.

## What is CPD?

CPD refers to the systematic maintenance, improvement, and broadening of knowledge and skills throughout a professional's career. Unlike traditional education, CPD is:

- **Self-directed** — You take ownership of your learning journey
- **Ongoing** — It continues throughout your entire career
- **Practice-focused** — It directly enhances your professional competence
- **Measurable** — Outcomes are tracked through credits, hours, or points

> *"CPD is not about collecting certificates — it is about continuously improving your ability to serve your clients, your organisation, and your profession."*

## CPD Requirements by Profession in Ghana

Different professional bodies in Ghana have varying CPD requirements. Here is a summary of key requirements:

| Profession | Regulatory Body | Annual CPD Requirement | Key Areas |
|------------|----------------|----------------------|-----------|
| **Accountants** | ICAG | 40–50 CPD hours | Auditing, tax, financial reporting, ethics |
| **Engineers** | GhIE | 30 CPD points | Technical updates, project management, safety |
| **Lawyers** | GLC / Ghana Bar Association | 12 CPD credits | Legal updates, ethics, practice management |
| **Medical Doctors** | MDC Ghana | 40 CPD credits | Clinical updates, patient safety, specialisation |
| **Nurses & Midwives** | NMC Ghana | 30 CPD hours | Clinical practice, leadership, patient care |
| **Pharmacists** | Pharmacy Council | 30 CPD points | Drug therapy, regulatory updates, ethics |
| **Architects** | ARB Ghana | 20 CPD points | Building standards, sustainability, design |
| **Quantity Surveyors** | QSAG | 25 CPD hours | Cost management, contracts, procurement |

*Note: Requirements may vary. Always check with your specific professional body for the most current guidelines.*

## Types of CPD Activities

CPD activities in Ghana are typically categorised into three types:

### Structured (Formal) Learning
- Accredited training courses and workshops
- Professional conferences and seminars
- University short courses and certificate programmes
- Online courses with assessments

### Self-Directed (Informal) Learning
- Reading professional journals and publications
- Participating in webinars and online forums
- Conducting research and publishing articles
- Mentoring junior professionals

### Work-Based Learning
- On-the-job training and secondments
- Participation in professional committees
- Development of new procedures or systems
- Peer review and quality assurance activities

## How to Track and Document Your CPD

Proper documentation is essential for CPD compliance. Here is a recommended system:

1. **Create a CPD log** — Track all activities with dates, hours, descriptions, and outcomes
2. **Save evidence** — Keep certificates, attendance records, and supporting materials
3. **Reflect on learning** — Document how each activity has improved your practice
4. **Align with your professional body** — Ensure activities meet their specific criteria

> **Pro Tip:** Many professional bodies in Ghana now accept digital CPD logs. Use a spreadsheet or dedicated CPD tracking platform to stay organised year-round.

## Common CPD Mistakes to Avoid

- **Procrastinating** — Trying to complete all CPD requirements in December is stressful and less effective
- **Ignoring your professional body's guidelines** — Not all CPD activities count; check first
- **Choosing quantity over quality** — One relevant, in-depth course is worth more than several superficial ones
- **Not aligning CPD with career goals** — Strategic CPD can accelerate your career progression
- **Failing to document properly** — If you cannot prove it, it did not happen
